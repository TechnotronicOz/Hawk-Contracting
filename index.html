About Hawk Contracting

As Owner of Hawk Contracting, I bring to the business 17 years of experience and leadership in the construction industry. I am a third generation construction worker. I pride myself on having superior principles of integrity, commitment, honesty, and quality. My goal is to ensure customer satisfaction and build long term relationships with clients. Hawk Contracting takes great pride in providing our construction services and fulfilling its clients wishes for the people and communities in which we live.

I am a family man. My wife and I have three beautiful children ranging in ages from 15 to 1. Knowing how chaotic life can get the family decided it was the best fit for “Daddy” to go out on his own to provide more flexibility for family time and to ensure the integrity of the work being completed is not distorted. I am involved in every stage of any project. I believe that success is a result of treating others fairly and with respect, hiring and retaining talented people, providing high quality work and never saying we can’t to a client.

- Steve Hawk, Owner
